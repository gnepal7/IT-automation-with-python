# Understand resume writing best practices
 - Information in resume must be attention grabing
 => To craft your resume, focus on:
 - Content
  . Demonstrate measurable results
  . Translate your experiences into transferable skills
  . Use impactful language
  . Add specific context
 - Structure
  . Keep in concise
  . Highlight your skills in a place they'll stand out
  . Follow a logical order and be scannable
 - Personalize
  . Include important words and phrease
  . Make deliberate decisions on what to include and exclude

# Craft a resume that gets you noticed
 - Use the X-Y-Z formula
  . X stands for your Accomplishment or Action: What did you specifically do? What was the task or project?
  . Y stands for the Quantifiable Result: What was the measurable outcome of your action? Use numbers, percentages, or concrete data to show the impact.
  . Z stands for the Method or Skills Used: How did you achieve this result? What specific skills or steps did you take?

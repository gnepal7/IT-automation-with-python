# Navigate interview formats
 - Behavioral interviews
  . Prepare and tailor your stories
  . Structure your answers clearly and emphasize your role
  . Be authentic and resourceful in your responses
 - Technical interviews
  . Solidify your technical knowledge and anticipate question types
  . Prepare to showcase your practical experience
  . Practice clear and adaptable technical communication
 - Case study interviews
  . Actively gather and clarify all case information
  . Demonstrate a structured, yet flexible, problem-solving approach
  . Conclude with a clear summary and well-reasoned recommendation

# Answer interview questions with the STAR method
 - STAR method
  . Situation
  . Task
  . Action
  . Result
